Creative Inquiry

Project Sol

During my sophmore year, I was part of a team that developed a cloud-based system to monitor solar radiation levels around the Clemson area. We developed sensors using Rasperry Pis equipped with photoresistors. These sensors published readings to an MQTT broker, which subsequently stored them in a Cloudant database. My primary role was the development of a R-Shiny application to display the collected data in an interactive website.
